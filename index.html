SNS Insights Collector

An internal, read-only analytics tool for the social media accounts we own and operate.

Internal use only Read-only Own accounts only

What it does

  • Collects daily statistics of our own authorized accounts — follower counts, video views, likes, comments and shares
  • Displays them on an internal reporting dashboard for our team

What it does NOT do

  • No posting, messaging, following or any write actions
  • No collection of data about other users, viewers or followers
  • Not offered to the general public — used only by our team on accounts we operate
