Privacy Policy — SNS Insights Collector

Last updated: July 10, 2026

SNS Insights Collector ("the App") is an internal analytics tool used solely by our own team to collect performance statistics of social media accounts that we own and operate.

1. Information We Collect

With the explicit authorization of the account holder, the App collects the following data from the authorized account only:

  • Basic profile information (display name, avatar)
  • Account statistics (follower count, following count, total likes, video count)
  • The list of videos posted by the account and their public performance metrics (view count, like count, comment count, share count)

The App does not collect any information about other users, viewers, commenters, or followers. It does not collect private messages, contact lists, or any content beyond the metrics listed above.

2. How We Use the Information

The collected data is used exclusively to build internal performance reports and dashboards for the accounts we operate. The data is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than internal analytics.

3. Data Storage and Sharing

Collected data is stored in access-controlled spreadsheets and internal systems available only to our team members. We do not sell, rent, or share the collected data with any third party.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained only as long as needed for internal reporting. Account holders may revoke the App's access at any time from their account settings, and may request deletion of previously collected data via the contact below. Deletion requests are handled within 30 days.

5. Contact

For any questions or data deletion requests regarding this policy, please contact: zuki1125@gmail.com
